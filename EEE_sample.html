Aspects of Electrical Engineering

Circuit Design

Focuses on designing circuits to achieve desired functionality in electrical devices and systems.

Control Systems

Encompasses the design and analysis of feedback systems for controlling the behavior of dynamic systems.

Power Generation

Centers on the processes and technologies used to generate electricity for societal needs.

Telecommunications

Studies the transmission of information over distances using electronic means.

Embedded Systems

Concerns the design of computer systems embedded within larger systems, optimized for specific applications.
